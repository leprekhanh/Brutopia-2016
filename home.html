WHAT IS WINTER LEADERSHIP CAMP?

Winter Leadership Camp is a weekend camp retreat focused on the core values of service leadership and how we can apply them in the real world. We have an awesome facilitator, Brian Ritchie, coming in to teach, guide, and help us discover more about ourselves as leaders and how we can create a culture of service leadership in every community we enter, whether it be in college, Circle K, the working world, or even for a community organization. We'll be posting Brian's introduction soon, so keep a look out for that!

Throughout our college experience, we are constantly developing and equipping ourselves with the necessary skills for the working world, such as public speaking, event planning, marketing, etc. However, we're rarely given the opportunity to develop ourselves as better leaders for our communities. No matter how many strategies or technical skills one has, understanding the core values of service leadership can't be something that is simply taught, but is a journey of self-reflection and understanding and bettering ourselves as individuals. As Michael Jackson says, "If you wanna make the world a better place; take a look at yourself, and then make a change."

That is what this event is about. We want to make this event an opportunity for you to cultivate your personal growth. Thus, this event isn't about your club or Circle K - this event is about YOU as an individual in a much larger community.

For this weekend, expect not only to learn more about yourself and leadership, but also to have FUN and create new and meaningful friendships as everyone attending this camp is on the same journey together. There will be tons of activities ranging from team-building, outdoors, and maybe even a late night bonfire! Did anyone say s'mores?

If you have any questions, please do not hesitate whatsoever to contact us. From the bottom of my heart, I truly hope everyone who attends camp has the most incredible experience possible, and I'm so excited to see what comes out of it. I hope you are, too!
