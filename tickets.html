HOW MUCH DOES WLC COST?

For the entire weekend, including 5 meals, lodging, and other event gear (note: transportation is not accounted for), the cost is $138 per person, and there will be no price increases. YAY. Please note that your payment must be RECEIVED by October 31st.

We understand that this is an expensive season, so with that, we are offering an installment plan for those who choose that route. With the installment plan, the first installment of $85 must be RECEIVED by October 31st to reserve your spot, and the second installment of $53 must be received by December 1st.
